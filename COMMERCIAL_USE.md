# Commercial Use of Multinex

Multinex is source-available for non-commercial research and educational use only.

Commercial use is not permitted under the public repository license.

Commercial use includes:

- company use
- startup use
- product integration
- SaaS/API deployment
- internal commercial R&D
- benchmarking for commercial decision-making
- paid consulting or client work
- training or deploying models for commercial advantage

Commercial access is available only through written permission, collaboration,
partnership, sponsored research, joint development, or a separate commercial
license.

Potential collaboration formats include:

- joint research collaboration
- industrial R&D partnership
- sponsored PhD/research project
- commercial licensing
- consulting agreement
- model adaptation for a specific commercial task
- co-authored publication or benchmark study
- revenue-sharing deployment agreement
